Our story

Alex and Jake met at university 9 years ago through a mutual friend on a night out when they should have been revising - Jake's crazy dance moves piqued Alex's interest - obviously! After a summer break and some texts back and forth they started back at uni and Jake got up the courage to ask Alex out on their first date to Giraffe in Cabot Circus.

Fast forward and you will have seen their journey to work with Giant Pandas, explore Asia and eventually settle down and create a life of their own in Sydney, Australia. Lots of challenges presented themselves and with that they grew together. After 7 long years Jake finally proposed to Alex in Cyprus.

May 2017 saw the relocation of Alex, Jake and Charlie the cat back to the UK outside of Southampton where they grew their fur family with two Chihuahuas, Keith and Molly.

Being the eager go-getters they are and organiser extraordinaires(!!!!) they felt it right to have a long 2 year engagement to plan the wedding of the year.

We hope you enjoy it!

FAQ

What are the details for Jake's 30th?

Jake's 30th is on the 2nd of September but we will be celebrating it on the 1st (the day after the Wedding). The plan is to have a BBQ at Box Farm, its a 10 min drive from the venue. Its a come and go event so don't feel pressured into staying the whole time, starts around 4pm and the BBQ will be going from around 6pm.

Should I pre-book a taxi in the evening?

There are many taxi companies in Glastonbury, so pre-booking isn't 100% neccesary, however we will be a minibus service in the evening dropping people back to their accomodation. The farm owner will be doing this so when your partied out just look for the minibus and let him know where you need to go.

Whats the drink situation?

Just help yourself to drinks, there will be plenty of wine on tables, champagne will be handed out for toasts, beer and spirits will be by the bar area.
