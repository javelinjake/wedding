Our story

Alex and Jake met at university 9 years ago through a mutual friend on a night out when we should have been revising - Jake's crazy dance moves piqued Alex's interest - obviously! After a summer break and some texts back and forth they started back at uni and Jake got up the courage to ask Alex out on their first date to Giraffe in Cabot Circus.

Fast forward and you will have seen their journey to work with Giant Pandas, explore Asia and eventually settle down and create a life of their own in Sydney, Australia. Lots of challenges presented themselves and with that they grew together. After 7 long years Jake finally proposed to Alex in Cyprus.

Being the eager go-getters they are and organiser extraordinaires(!!!!) they felt it right to have a long 2 year engagement to plan the wedding of the year.

We hope you enjoy it!
